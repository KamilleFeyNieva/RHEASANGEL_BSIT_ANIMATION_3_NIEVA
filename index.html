BIOGRAPHY

Hello! I'm Kamille Fey S. Nieva, a BSIT major in Animation. Borned in Minto Guinobatan, Albay. I am a creative traditional and digital artist. When I'm not preoccupied with illustration, you can find me exploring new art such painting and photography or just reading novels and watching movie, anime, and donghua. What got me interested in painting and photography is because of the urged to record beautiful scenes and meaningful events. Illustrating has always been my favorite, where not only I could record what I see in a form of visual art but also making dreams and imagination appear in reality.
